Life In Reverse

A terminal-based Python program that analyzes your current habits and shows how they may be preventing you from reaching your goal — instead of just telling you how to succeed.

Built as my final project for Stanford Code in Place 2026.


What It Does


You choose a goal category — Education, Career, Health & Fitness, Skill Learning, or Personal Project
You answer 5 questions about your current habits
The program calculates a Failure Risk Score from 0–100
You get a report showing your risk level and the exact habits working against you
Optionally, you get a personalised 30-day roadmap with specific fixes for your weaknesses



Risk Levels

Score      Level
0–25      LOW
26–50     MODERATE
51–75     HIGH
76–100    CRITICAL


How to Run:

python main.py

No installs. No dependencies. Just Python.
